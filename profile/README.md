# Myaza

Cross-border payment infrastructure for African businesses. Built on Stellar USDC rails with multi-chain support.

Myaza gives merchants, platforms, and fintechs the infrastructure to send, receive, and settle cross-border transactions across Africa. Stablecoin-native. API-first. Compliant by design.

---

## What is Myaza?

Myaza (by Flitstack Technologies) is stablecoin-powered cross-border payment infrastructure for African markets. We sit beneath the apps and platforms your customers already use, handling FX conversion, corridor routing, compliance, and settlement.

Built on Stellar USDC rails with multi-chain support, Myaza lets businesses move money across African corridors using stablecoin speed on regulated infrastructure.

We are not a consumer wallet. We are the rails.

---

## Who is Myaza for?

- **Merchants** accepting cross-border payments from African customers
- **Fintechs and neobanks** that need embedded payment infrastructure
- **Platforms and marketplaces** disbursing to suppliers, vendors, or gig workers across borders
- **Enterprises** managing cross-border payroll, treasury, or supplier payments

---

## Key Features

- **Stablecoin-native settlement:** USDC-denominated rails via Stellar, with multi-chain support for speed and cost
- **Multi-corridor routing:** intelligent routing across African payment corridors with live FX rates
- **Multi-currency support:** convert and settle across local African currencies without manual intervention
- **AML/KYC compliance engine:** transaction monitoring, risk scoring, and step-up authentication built for African regulatory environments
- **API-first architecture:** integrate directly into your product with clean, documented APIs
- **Virtual card issuance:** issue and manage virtual cards for online business payments
- **Low fees:** competitive corridor rates with transparent, predictable pricing

## How to Integrate

1. **Register your business** at [business.myaza.co](https://business.myaza.co)
2. **Complete KYB** — submit your entity details and compliance documents
3. **Get API credentials** — access your API keys and sandbox environment
4. **Integrate** — connect your platform to Myaza's payment and FX endpoints
5. **Go live** — enable corridors and start processing

For individual users onboarding through a Myaza-powered product:

1. Sign up via the app or web platform
2. Complete KYC verification
3. Fund your wallet and transact across borders

## Architecture

- **Stellar network** — primary settlement layer for USDC-denominated transactions
- **Multi-chain support** — additional chain integrations for corridor flexibility
- **Compliance engine** — five-tier risk scoring, 35+ AML rules across seven categories, BigQuery audit logging
- **Go** — backend services and transaction processing
- **Node.js** — API gateway, integrations, and auxiliary services
- **Flutter** — cross-platform mobile and web client applications
- **AWS** — cloud infrastructure deployed via Kamal 2
